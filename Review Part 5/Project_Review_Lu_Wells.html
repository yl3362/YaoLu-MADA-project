Further comments

Overall this project is very well thought out. It has some very good points and lots of coding work went into its completion. The manuscript is a little jumbled and needs some reorganization. The summary/abstract should be a short paragraph that gives an overall description of your project. The introduction should include descriptions of previous work with the sources you cited and then a description of the data and how you got it. The codebook is very helpful in understanding what the variables are but I think it could be stored in a separate file. The methods could also include a short description of the steps you took to clean, explore, and analyze your data. There are also some instances using “we” in the manuscript where you should use “I”.
